INTERVIEW PREPARATION NOTES
Course: Career Development and Professional Skills

===== TECHNICAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION =====

Common Python Questions:
- Explain the difference between lists and tuples
- What are decorators in Python?
- How does memory management work in Python?
- Explain list comprehensions with examples
- What is the difference between deep copy and shallow copy?

Data Structures Questions:
- Explain time complexity of common operations
- When to use arrays vs linked lists?
- How does a hash table work?
- Explain binary search tree operations
- What is the difference between stack and queue?

Machine Learning Basics:
- Supervised vs unsupervised learning
- Explain overfitting and underfitting
- What is cross-validation?
- Common evaluation metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, F1)
- Explain bias-variance tradeoff

Git and Version Control:
- Basic Git commands (clone, commit, push, pull)
- What is branching and merging?
- How to resolve merge conflicts?
- Best practices for commit messages
- Explain pull requests and code reviews

===== BEHAVIORAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION =====

STAR Method (Situation, Task, Action, Result):
Use this framework to answer behavioral questions

Common Behavioral Questions:
1. Tell me about yourself
   - Brief background, education, skills
   - Recent projects and achievements
   - Career goals and interests
   - Why interested in this role

2. Why should we hire you?
   - Highlight relevant skills
   - Show enthusiasm and cultural fit
   - Mention specific achievements
   - Connect your goals with company mission

3. Describe a challenging project
   - Explain the problem clearly
   - Your approach and solution
   - Obstacles faced and how you overcame them
   - Results and lessons learned

4. How do you handle deadlines?
   - Time management strategies
   - Prioritization techniques
   - Example of meeting tight deadline
   - How you handle pressure

5. Describe a time you worked in a team
   - Your role in the team
   - How you collaborated
   - Conflict resolution (if any)
   - Team's achievement

===== PROJECT DISCUSSION TIPS =====

When discussing projects:
- Start with problem statement
- Explain your role clearly
- Discuss technologies used and why
- Highlight challenges and solutions
- Mention measurable results
- Be ready for technical deep-dive
- Have GitHub links ready

===== COMPANY RESEARCH =====

Before interview, research:
- Company's products and services
- Recent news and achievements
- Company culture and values
- Tech stack they use
- Competitors and market position

===== QUESTIONS TO ASK INTERVIEWER =====

Good questions to ask:
- What does a typical day look like?
- What projects would I work on?
- What is the team structure?
- What are the growth opportunities?
- What technologies does the team use?
- What is the onboarding process?
- What are the biggest challenges facing the team?

===== TECHNICAL SKILLS TO HIGHLIGHT =====

For AI/ML Roles:
- Python programming proficiency
- Data preprocessing and cleaning
- Model training and evaluation
- Understanding of algorithms
- Experience with ML libraries
- Problem-solving approach
- Documentation skills

For Software Development:
- Programming language expertise
- Data structures and algorithms
- Database knowledge
- Version control (Git)
- API development
- Testing and debugging
- Code documentation

===== SOFT SKILLS TO EMPHASIZE =====

- Communication (written and verbal)
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Problem-solving mindset
- Adaptability and learning agility
- Time management
- Attention to detail
- Initiative and proactivity

===== COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID =====

- Not researching the company
- Speaking negatively about past experiences
- Being unprepared for technical questions
- Not asking questions
- Poor body language (for in-person)
- Not following up after interview
- Exaggerating skills or experience

===== FOLLOW-UP BEST PRACTICES =====

After Interview:
- Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- Mention specific discussion points
- Reiterate your interest
- Keep it brief and professional
- Connect on LinkedIn (if appropriate)

===== SALARY NEGOTIATION TIPS =====

- Research market rates
- Know your worth
- Consider total compensation (not just salary)
- Be prepared to justify your ask
- Stay professional and positive
- Don't accept immediately - ask for time to consider

===== REMOTE INTERVIEW TIPS =====

- Test technology beforehand
- Ensure good lighting and background
- Dress professionally
- Minimize distractions
- Have notes ready (but don't read)
- Maintain eye contact with camera
- Have backup plan for technical issues
